About Me

Hi, I'm Savannah, a professional tattoo artist dedicated to designing a unique tattoo that reflects your personality and story. With three years of experience, I specialize in blackwork, American traditional, illustrative, fine line, and script.

I’m a traveler at heart, so I'm always outdoors hiking, going to shows, or enjoying city life. My joy in traveling has inspired me to tattoo in multiple places; as of now, I tattoo in Colorado full-time and guest spot in North Carolina. Regardless of where you are and whether it’s your first tattoo or a new addition to your collection, I strive to provide a comfortable and welcoming environment for all my clients. I look forward to turning your ideas into a stunning piece of art!
